About Me

Patrick is a full stack web developer who helps companies of all sizes maximize their customer base by developing comprehensible web pages using the industries most sought after techniques and languages. Patrick understands the neccessity of a user friendly interface and the impact it can have on the transition from website visitors to client customers. With over 5 years in customer service, Patrick is an expert with working with people and can provide web pages that are easily understood and well developed. Patrick has had an intensive full stack web development education through the University of Arizona where he has learned programming languages such as HTML,CSS,Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap along with others. With this education he is well qualified to provide high quality work on front end or back end web development. Along with his Education from the University of Arizona, Patrick has also obtained his Bachelors degree in Communication through Arizona State University.
